# **My Own Problems**

### Right bias, wrong entry
>_I would find myself with the correct directional bias BUT as soon as I enter the trade, I get stopped out by a few ticks or 1-2 points and then price heads to my target... DAFUQ!?_

### Invalid invalidation points
>_When I would place my stops at points of "invalidation", it actually isn't the case..._

### Patience
>_Sometimes I would not recognise that my initial trades come from the thought of "forcing" trades to work rather than letting it happen. The idea of "getting it done quickly" destroyed my patience._

### Reacting to pops
>_I have a personality trait called 'sensory processing sentitivity' which pretty much makes my nervous system fire off at the smallest stimuli. I would react to majority of the sudden bids/offers that would enter the market and the sudden shift it would cause._
