# **Supply and Demand**

## Explaination
Supply and demand is the core principal of trading. Basically if the price of an object is thought to be at a: 

- **Discount => DEMAND**
- **Premium => SUPPLY**

These levels are constantly shifting with the market regime and sentiment. When sudden influencing factors (like macro events) impact the market you will see this shift a lot faster as confidence is either gained or lost.

## My own interpretation
1. **Zones**: These are HTF levels that you capture using any timeframe greater than 30M. These are usually well established levels or pivot points. I would normally pair this with PbD volume model as confirmation. If volume aligns with these found "_zones_" then more times than most, it will be a conclusive/deterministic area of auction.

2. **Interest**: In many cases, there will always be levels where auctions occurs that are not at these "zones". These levels can appear under other circumstances and suddenly become levels where you see lots of buying and selling interaction. I refer to these levels as "_interest_".

I find that differentiating these types of S&D will allow you to further contextualise the market, so that bias is not so definitive and one dimensional. Combining these levels with volume is key.

## Locating these levels
The method I use to locate these S&D _zones_ are from the resource videos. Just give them a watch and you'll understand.

However, locating S&D _interest_ is different. Look for repeated historic behavior around the similar levels (rejection/pivot points) and then analyze it with the following: 
* Volume profile concepts i.e. POCs, HVNs, VAs
* Rolling volume profiles i.e. 30 day and 5 day
* Key psychological levels e.g. 7000 or 6800 for ES and 20000 for NQ

## Validating these levels
I look to volume profile concepts and order flow to validate historic and current price action. Ideally, you would like to see a battle e.g. lots of buyers hitting the bid at a supply zone and sellers able to absorb and push price away. 

## Level rotation
If a S&D level is broken, it is possible that that rotation will occur and the opposing side will begin to actively defend this level:
- **Broken SUPPLY + dominating BUYERS = new DEMAND**
- **Broken DEMAND + dominating SELLERS = new SUPPLY**

It is important to note that a broken level can break down but then be reclaimed in the following day/session. 

## Trading with this concept
Without going into advanced discretion, to trade with this concept you simply have to wait for price to go to these _zones_ or _interest_ areas and watch the battle. 

It is not guaranteed that price will always head to these levels so if you are starting out, just sit on your hands and wait.
